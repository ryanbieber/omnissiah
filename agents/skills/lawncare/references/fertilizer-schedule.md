# Lawn Fertilization Schedule - Iowa Waverly

## Optimal Application Schedule

| Task | Month | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| **Spring Fertilization** | April-May | Apply after grass greens up (80-100 lbs N/1000 sq ft annually) |
| **Early Summer** | June | Second application if using split schedule |
| **Late Summer/Early Fall** | August-September | Most important application - builds winter hardiness |
| **Fall Fertilization** | September-October | Apply before dormancy for spring green-up |

## Best Practices

- **Timing:** Apply when soil temp is 55-70°F consistently
- **Type:** Use slow-release nitrogen formulas for cool-season grasses
- **Frequency:** 2-4 applications per year
- **Amount:** 0.5-1 lb nitrogen per 1000 sq ft per application
- **Weather:** Apply before rain (within 48 hours) or water in after

## Recommended Fertilizers for Iowa Lawns

- **Late Spring/Summer:** 10-20-20 or 12-8-8 blend
- **Fall:** Higher potassium blend (e.g., 10-10-20) for winter strength
- **Avoid:** Heavy nitrogen in summer (causes disease stress)

## Seasonal Nitrogen Budget

For cool-season grasses in Iowa, allocate annual nitrogen:

- **April-May (Spring):** 25-30% of annual N (greening and growth)
- **June-July (Summer):** 0-15% of annual N (drought stress risk, apply sparingly)
- **August-September (Late Summer/Early Fall):** 40-50% of annual N (most critical - winter prep)
- **September-October (Fall):** 10-20% of annual N (final push, lower rates)

## Application Methods

- **Broadcast Spreader:** For large areas, even coverage
- **Drop Spreader:** Better control, less drift near beds
- **Liquid Application:** Fast absorption, good for micronutrients
- **Granular:** Slow-release, longer-lasting effects

## Post-Application Care

1. **Watering:** Water in within 48 hours if no rain
2. **Traffic:** Avoid heavy foot traffic for 24-48 hours
3. **Mowing:** Can mow 2-3 days after application
4. **Monitoring:** Watch for signs of disease or stress

## Safety

- Store in cool, dry place
- Follow label directions for pets and children
- Wear protective equipment during application
- Dispose of containers according to local regulations
